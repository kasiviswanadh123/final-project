Heart-Healthy Nutrition Plan

Fatty Fish

Rich in omega-3 fatty acids that help reduce inflammation and lower triglycerides in the blood.

Benefits:
  • Lowers risk of arrhythmias
  • Reduces blood pressure
  • Decreases triglyceride levels

Whole Grains

High in fiber which helps lower cholesterol and reduce the risk of heart disease.

Benefits:
  • Regulates blood pressure
  • Improves blood cholesterol
  • Helps maintain healthy weight

Berries

Packed with antioxidants like anthocyanins that protect against oxidative stress and inflammation.

Benefits:
  • Reduces LDL cholesterol
  • Improves artery function
  • Lowers blood pressure

Leafy Greens

High in vitamin K which helps protect arteries and promote proper blood clotting.

Benefits:
  • Rich in dietary nitrates
  • Reduces arterial stiffness
  • Improves endothelial function

Avocados

Excellent source of monounsaturated fats linked to reduced cholesterol levels.

Benefits:
  • Lowers LDL cholesterol
  • Raises HDL cholesterol
  • Reduces risk of metabolic syndrome

Olive Oil

Rich in antioxidants and monounsaturated fatty acids that protect heart health.

Benefits:
  • Lowers risk of heart disease
  • Reduces inflammation
  • Protects against LDL oxidation

Heart-Healthy Workouts

Brisk Walking

A simple yet effective cardiovascular exercise that strengthens your heart and improves circulation.

Recommendation:
  • 30 minutes daily
  • Moderate pace (3-4 mph)
  • Can be done anywhere

Swimming

A full-body workout that's easy on the joints while providing excellent cardiovascular benefits.

Recommendation:
  • 30-45 minutes, 3-4x/week
  • Various strokes for variety
  • Great for all fitness levels

Cycling

Low-impact exercise that helps lower resting heart rate and burn calories effectively.

Recommendation:
  • 30-60 minutes, 3-5x/week
  • Moderate intensity
  • Can be outdoor or stationary

Interval Training

Alternating between high and low intensity to maximize cardiovascular benefits in less time.

Recommendation:
  • 20-30 minutes, 2-3x/week
  • 1:2 work:rest ratio
  • Great for improving VO2 max

Strength Training

Builds muscle which helps burn more calories and improves metabolic health.

Recommendation:
  • 2-3x per week
  • 8-12 reps per exercise
  • Focus on major muscle groups

Yoga

Combines physical postures with breathing exercises to reduce stress and improve flexibility.

Recommendation:
  • 30-60 minutes, 2-3x/week
  • Focus on breath control
  • Great for stress reduction
